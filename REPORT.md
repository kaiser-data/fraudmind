## FLAGGED findings (deterministic control violations)

### F001 — CRITICAL: Ratio Consulting GmbH — self-approved vendor, €295,120 paid, zero deliverables
- **MV-U05 created AND approved** vendor 209101 "Ratio Consulting GmbH" incl. bank details on
  12.05.2025 — four-eyes violation of the documented vendor-master control
  (`Stammdatenaenderungen_2025.csv:row8`).
- Permissions matrix shows MV-U05 (Einkauf) holds the **toxic SoD combination**
  Buchen + Zahlungslauf + Stammdaten/Kreditor anlegen, and NO approval right
  (`Berechtigungsauswertung_2025.xlsx#Berechtigungen`).
- **5 invoices ER901416–ER901420** "Beratungsleistungen lt. Rahmenvertrag", total **€295,120
  gross**, each **paid exactly 2 days** after invoice date; **no goods receipt or delivery
  evidence** for any of them (`Lieferantenbuchungen.txt:rows2553–2562`).
- Vendor sits directly next to related parties in the 209xxx block (209113 = parent company
  Muster Beteiligungs GmbH Wien per Gesellschafterliste). Pattern: JET criterion K1 + K2.

### F002 — CRITICAL: Unrecorded liabilities €192,000 (cut-off, K4)
8 invoices in `Fakturajournal_Januar_2026_Kreditoren.csv:rows2–9` are dated Jan 2026 but carry
**service dates 19.–27.12.2025** ("Frachten Dez 2025", "Energie Dez 2025", …). None of the 8
vendors has ANY 2025 booking in the AP subledger → no accrual was recognized in FY2025.
Expenses understated / profit overstated by **€192,000**.
- €22,000 Nord Transport, €38,000 Delta Energie, €41,000 Atlas Werkstoffe, €17,000 Vega Technik,
  €26,000 Orion Logistik, €14,500 Kronos Druck, €20,500 Prisma Chemie, €13,000 Helios Systeme.

### F010 — HIGH: The same 8 vendors were created past the control (ghost vendors)
Vendors 209130–209137 exist in the vendor master (`Lieferanten.txt:rows132–139`) with the
placeholder city "Musterort", have **no creation entry** in the master-data change log (the
documented, approval-required process used for 209101/209112), no opening balance, and no 2025
activity. The creation control was bypassed — corroborates F002 as deliberate.

### F014 (corroboration) — the ER number sequence gap is exactly these invoices

in the Jan-2026 journal: the numbers were issued but kept out of FY2025 — independent
confirmation of the cut-off scheme from a third data source.

## Deliberately NOT flagged (false-positive protection)

- **Weekend postings**: 28.5% of ALL GL entries are weekend-entered — routine for this company
  (F018 INFO documents the baseline). Time-based flags require the pattern to be rare (<5%).
- **Near-limit payment pairs months apart** (e.g. Ost Kunststoff, Nova Recycling): normal
  business, excluded by the 7-day clustering rule.
- **Subledger ↔ GL ↔ OP-list tie-outs**: AR (€13,412,543.05) and AP (−€13,208,855) reconcile
  exactly to control accounts 230000/330000 and the provided Abstimmung — verified, no finding.
- **Credit-limit report**: all AUSNUTZUNG values match computed year-end balances — verified.
- **Journal approval log**: all 77xxxxx entry numbers of GJ/AB journals appear in the
  Freigabe-Log, no ERSTELLER==FREIGEBER rows, all "Freigegeben" — control operated.

## Method (one paragraph)

Deterministic reconciliation and control-testing rules run over the fully-linked transaction
graph (invoice → goods list → subledger → GL → approval log → next-period payments; 2,040/2,041
sales invoices fully chained). Rules decide; no LLM decides a number. Thresholds and control
definitions are read from the dossier's own audit-planning workpaper, checks self-calibrate
against the dossier's own base rates, and every finding carries row/page-level provenance —
the same pipeline runs unchanged on a new dossier folder
(`python3 ingest.py <path> && python3 checks.py <path>`).
